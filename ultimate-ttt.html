How it works
This whole game is one HTML file. When you Create a game your phone builds a connection offer; your friend's Join builds an answer. Trading those two codes lets the phones talk directly (peer-to-peer) with no server in the middle. Public STUN servers are used only to discover your address for the initial handshake — no game data passes through them. If the connection drops, tap Reconnect and re-trade codes; your board is preserved and re-synced automatically.
